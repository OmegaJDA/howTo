Breakfast Dinner Dessert Bread Soup

Breads

White Bread

Ingredients

  • 11 c. flour
  • 2 T. yeast
  • 6 T. sugar
  • 6 T. oil
  • 4 T. salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment: kettle, large mixing bowl, large mixing spoon (wooden), 4cup glass measuring cup, platic wrap etc.

Steps

  1. Put water in a kettle and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all other dry ingredients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into large glass measuring cup.
  4. Start adding the 3 cups of hot water. When you only have half a cup left, feel the water and keep stir.
  5. etc.
